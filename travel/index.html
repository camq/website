Backpacking Through Europe

In April 2016 I spent 31 days traveling through Europe visiting 15 different countries

London, England

Days spent: 2
nights spent: 1
Places visited: 221 Baker Street, Hyde Park, Regents Park, West End, Wembly, Big Ben

This was my first time in London by myself, although I have visited before many times with my family. I rented a Borris Bike and rode around London. This is the city in which I started and ended my journey

Paris, France

Days spent: 1
nights spent: 0
Places visited: Eiffle Tower, Jardins du Trocadéro, L'Arc de Triomphe

Brussels was intended to be the second country I visited, however, because the city was still recovering from the terror attacks (March 22, 2016), I decided to go to Paris insted. Arriving by bus at approximitly 6:00am I took the metro to the center of town and walked around. In the evening I took a train to Nice
